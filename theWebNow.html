The modern web
the evolution of the internet from 2007 to now

an overview.

Over the past 8 years, the world wide web has seen incredible growth and change. With the smartphone invasion, social media revolution, and ceaseless opportunities for the average joe to take a crack at being a developer, there's no slowing down in sight!

This site seeks to showcase the past 8 years and just how far our digital world has come.
